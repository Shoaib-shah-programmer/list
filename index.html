Delecious chicken biryani recipe

ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

steps to prepare

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.v
  2. Cook rice until 70% done, drain and keep aside.
  3. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the whole spices, then add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft, then add the marinated chicken and cook until done.
  6. In a large pot, layer half the rice, then chicken, then the remaining rice.
  7. Top with saffron milk, mint, and coriander leaves.
  8. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.
  9. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.

key term

basmati rice
A long-grain, aromatic rice traditionally used in Indian cooking.
biryani masala
A spice mix specially made for biryani, including ingredients like cumin, coriander, and cardamom.
dum biryani
A slow cooking method where food is cooked in its own steam by sealing the pot.
